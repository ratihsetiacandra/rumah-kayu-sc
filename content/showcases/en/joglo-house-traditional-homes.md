## Joglo Project Documentation by Rumah Kayu SC

The Joglo is the pinnacle of Javanese wooden architecture. Its distinctive four-pillared structure (*saka guru*) and soaring pyramidal roof create spaces that feel both grand and intimate.

Our Joglo projects range from faithfully restored antique pieces with intricate hand-carved details to contemporary interpretations that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern living needs. Each one tells a story of heritage, built to last for generations.

### Featured Projects

- **Antique White Joglo** — A beautifully restored Joglo with white-painted carved doors, a Ganesha statue at the entrance, and lush green gardens creating a serene tropical retreat.
- **Natural Wood Joglo** — An elevated Joglo with a raised terrace, wooden seating, and traditional Javanese carved doors showcasing the natural beauty of aged teak.
- **Open Joglo Pavilion** — A versatile open-air Joglo with supporting pillars, antique blue fence details, stone totem statues, and a classic red-tiled pyramidal roof.
