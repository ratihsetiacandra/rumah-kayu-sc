## Project: Balyand Lifestyle & Kopi Teras Sawah, Bali

This project showcases how traditional Javanese wood construction can transform commercial venues. From the soaring Joglo rooflines of Balyand Lifestyle to the intimate wooden interiors of Kopi Teras Sawah, every detail is crafted to create spaces that customers remember.

The combination of antique wood textures, warm lighting, and open-air design creates an atmosphere that modern materials simply cannot replicate. Both venues sit along a scenic Bali road surrounded by rice paddies, with the wooden architecture blending naturally into the tropical landscape.

### Highlights

- **Balyand Lifestyle** — A two-storey wooden venue with an open terrace, railing details, and hanging plants that create a welcoming tropical entrance.
- **Kopi Teras Sawah** — Features a stunning interior bar with hand-carved ceiling panels, ambient spotlighting, and rattan seating facing panoramic glass windows overlooking the rice fields.
- Traditional Joglo red-tiled pyramidal roofs with hanging lanterns and lush greenery at the entrance.
